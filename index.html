From Me, Guide to practice Hiragana

Learning Hiragana is the first step to unlocking the Japanese language! Here are some helpful tips to help you master them quickly and fun!

1. Understand the "A-I-U-E-O" Order

Japanese follows a very consistent vowel pattern: A, I, U, E, O.

2. Practice Writing (Stroke Order)

Japanese characters are meant to be written in a specific order. Writing them out by hand helps your brain remember the shapes.

3. Daily Flashcard Rounds

Learn around 5-10 characters per day. Use the Shuffle button frequently.
